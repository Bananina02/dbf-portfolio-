about company

Our team of transport engineers successfully designs and implements worldwide transports of equipment and capital goods for the plant construction, power industry, mining, oil and gas and chemical industry.

Today DBF LOJİSTİK ANONİM ŞİRKETİ with its team of dedicated professionals, various transport partners, assets owners and partner terminals offers customers full service of integrated project logistics service provider.
