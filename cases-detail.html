Delivery of heavy equipment for the chemical plant from Russia to Turkey

client
PJSC TATNEFT
PERIOD
September 2022 - March 2023
PROJECT
Delivery of heavy equipment for the chemical plant from Russia to Turkey
CARGO
Total volume more than 10,000 freight tons, including two reactor facilities, each weighing more than 340 tons, 9,619 mm long, 6,995 m in diameter.

scope of work

Stage 1

Road transportation by modular trailers from temporary storage warehouses in Almetyevsk and Nizhnekamsk to the port of Nizhnekamsk (Republic of Tatarstan)

Stage 2

Loading of equipment onto barges by the "RO-RO" method and crane equipment;

Stage 3

Water transportation along the rivers Kama, Volga, Don, Azov and Black Seas to the port of Tuzla of the Republic of Turkey;

Stage 4

Unloading of equipment by the Ro-Ro method and crane equipment in the port of Tuzla, Republic of Turkey;

Stage 5

Construction of temporary bypass roads, some of which had a grade of more than 13%;

Stage 6

Development by engineers of special retaining structures for lashing of reactors on a modular trailer;

Stage 7

Road transportation on modular trailers to the construction site of the chemical plant in the industrial zone "Gebkim";

Stage 8

Rigging of equipment and installation of reactor facilities in the design position.
